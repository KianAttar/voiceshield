Drag and drop

Drop one or multiple video files onto the app. Supports MP4, MKV, MOV, and WebM.

Three presets

Subtle, Moderate, or Strong anonymization. Pick the level that fits your needs.

Advanced controls

Fine-tune pitch, formant shift, and add robotic or echo effects for maximum disguise.

Completely offline

Your files never leave your computer. All audio processing runs locally via FFmpeg.

macOS users

Since VoiceShield is not signed with an Apple Developer certificate, macOS may show an "app is damaged" warning. To fix this, open Terminal and run:

xattr -cr /Applications/VoiceShield.app

Then open the app normally. This only needs to be done once.
